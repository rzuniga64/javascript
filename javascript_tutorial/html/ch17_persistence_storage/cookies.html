HTML Cookies

Cookies are not evil!

  • Cookies are specific to a domain, e.g. domain1.com
  • and, optionally, a path on that domain, e.g. domain1.com/path1/
  • Cookies can expire at a set date and time
  • or when the browser is closed (session cookie)
  • Cookies can be SECURE
  • Domains are limited to 20 cookies
  • No cookie can be greater that 4Kb
